NZQR

The New Zealand Qualifications Resourcing is an unofficial platform created for students within the New Zealand education system.

Its mission is simple: provide free, high-quality educational material for the students of Aotearoa, New Zealand. Not only does this allow for students to best prepare for assessments, but it also access to content that is outside any formal school they are enrolled in. Fot too long, the New Zealand system has been fractured and confusing, now, content is synthesised here. For all.
